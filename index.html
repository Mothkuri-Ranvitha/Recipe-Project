Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk powder
  • Ghee
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar

Recipe

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Add ghee and milk to form dough.
  3. Deep fry balls until golden brown.
  4. Boil sugar, water, and cardamom for syrup.
  5. Soak fried balls in syrup.

Kheer Recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom
  • Nuts

Recipe

  1. Boil rice in milk.
  2. Add sugar and cardamom.
  3. Cook until thick.
  4. Garnish with nuts.

Motichoor Laddoo

Ingredients

  • Besan (gram flour)
  • Ghee
  • Sugar

Recipe

  1. Roast besan in ghee.
  2. Make sugar syrup.
  3. Mix roasted besan with syrup.
  4. Shape into balls.
